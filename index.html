About

Neblina

Neblina was born in Lisbon in the 90's. From a young age Hip Hop culture became an influence in her life. Her first original song was made in 2012 as well as her Mixtape "Última Ceia", although these projects were never released. In 2020 Neblina took the next step in her music, participating in both national and international projects and is currently working on her first EP. Her lyrics are mainly inspired by her own experience as an LGBTQIA+ person and aim to fight for LGBTQIA+ rights.
